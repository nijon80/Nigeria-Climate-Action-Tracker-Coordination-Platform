Legal & Strategic Frameworks

Climate Change Act 2021

The primary legislation providing the comprehensive legal framework for all climate change actions in Nigeria.

National Climate Policy (NCCP)

Aims to embed climate change responses into the heart of all national planning and budgeting processes.

Long-Term Low Emission Development Strategies

Communicating long-term low greenhouse gas emissions development strategies in line with Paris Agreement aims.

Energy Transition Plan (ETP)

Strategic roadmap to reduce fossil fuel dependency and enhance the renewable energy mix in the national grid.

Green Bond Program

Financing mechanism for climate-aligned projects in renewable energy, afforestation, and sustainable transport.

NASPA-CCN Strategy

The National Adaptation Strategy focused on enhancing resilience across sectors against adverse climate impacts.

National Environmental Policy

Core policy promoting sustainable development through ecosystem conservation and environmental protection.

Great Green Wall Initiative

Large-scale initiative to combat desertification, land degradation, and create green employment in the North.

Erosion & Flood Policy

Mitigation policy addressing the significant climate-related challenges of seasonal flooding and land erosion.

Renewable Energy & Efficiency Policy (REEEP)

Dedicated policy to promote renewable energy adoption and increase energy efficiency standards nationwide.

National Forest Policy

Directs the sustainable management of Nigeria's forest resources to enhance sequestration and biodiversity.
